Our Commitment to You

For Fosters and Adopters

Here’s what you will get from us: PAWS offers you an easy-to-use platform to search for and adopt pets from shelters. You’ll be able to connect with foster families, learn about the animals, and make an informed decision on adoption.

For Rescue Organizations and Veterinarians

Here’s what you will get from us: PAWS provides tools to simplify your work, allowing you to manage pet listings, track adoption processes, and connect with potential fosters and adopters. Our goal is to make the adoption process as smooth as possible for everyone involved.

Meet Sarah and Bella

When I adopted Bella through PAWS, I never imagined how much she would change my life. The platform made it so easy to connect with her foster family, see pictures of her, and learn about her playful personality before we even met. Scheduling a meet-and-greet and getting advice from a rescue organization through PAWS gave me the confidence to bring her home.

Bella was shy at first, but within days, she became my best friend. We go on long walks, and she’s always there to greet me with excitement when I come home. Adopting through PAWS was seamless, and I’m forever grateful for the support they provided.

Now, I volunteer with PAWS, helping other pets like Bella find their forever homes. It’s amazing to see how many lives are changed, just like mine was.
